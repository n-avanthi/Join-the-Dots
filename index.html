Join The Dots

Gameplay:
a. Players draw lines to connect dots of the same color.
b. Players can connect any two same-colored dots but must ultimately achieve the target number of connections of the specified color within the given time limit

Rules:
a. Only same-colored dots can be connected.
b. Connections must be orthogonal (up, down, left, right); no diagonal lines allowed.
c. Lines cannot intersect or cross over other dots
